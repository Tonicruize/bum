Packed with cutting-edge features

ATM is the first ever on-chain and off-chain trading system. Offering the ability to use on chain data and off chain events and behaviors to trigger Buys and Sells on the Blockchain. Real-time trading

ATM will trade in real-time using both on and off-chain data to trigger buys and sells on Solana.

Advanced charts

Each user who deploys an ATM will have advanced tracking to be able to monitor, maintain and mange their results.

Enterprise grade security

The ATM is built with security ranking as the utmost important focus for the team. All security metrics have been met along with a full audit of the platform. Our Partner Certik will be auditing all aspects of the Platform.

Real World Integrations

The ATM will have unlimited possible integrations into the logic for triggering buys and sells. Integrate Telegram, X, TikTok, PolyMarket, Reddit, Whatsapp and virtually anything.

Pre Made Strategies

The ATM will have built in preset strategies. If you are new to trading or want the logic to just work for you then you can select one. If you are advanced you can make a manual strategy.
